# Multi Layered Architecture Really Matter for Enterprise System

Multi layered backend architecture pattern is the most popular choice for an enterprise system due to the data integrity, scaleability, interoperability and manageability from the development perspective.

Unlike start-up product which require fast development to penetrate the market, enterprise style platform will prefer to have slow development cycle while ensuring stability and data integrity. Resulting in complex architetural layer. 

Each layer will has their own purposes and ensuring data flow between layer are passed safely from security, performance and integrity perspective
