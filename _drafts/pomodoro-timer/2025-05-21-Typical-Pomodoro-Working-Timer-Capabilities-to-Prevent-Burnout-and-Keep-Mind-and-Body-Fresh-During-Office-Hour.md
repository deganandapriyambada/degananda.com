Pomodoro technique is a working hour governance framework which mainly used by employee to prevent exhaustion from work stress & pressure by keeping the mind fresh within the working hour.

# Pomodoro Princple

There are three main principles of pomodoro technique

1. Working time must be capped on 25 minutes.
2. Fully concertrate during the 25 minutes work time, no multi tasking (depend on the role)
3. Breaktime after 25 mins is mandatory and the duration of breaktime must be increased after fourth iteration 

Pomodoro platform must be able to support the user to apply these principle on their working time and often combined with gamifaction system to encourage the user to adopt pomodoro quickly.

## End to End Pomodoro Lifecycle

Pomodoro activities is started all the way back before even executing work. Task planning and scheduling prior to the work start are also part of the entire lifecycle up to the work closing.
