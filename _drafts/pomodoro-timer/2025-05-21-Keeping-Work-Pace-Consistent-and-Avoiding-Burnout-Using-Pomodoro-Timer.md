# Effect of Burnout on Workplaces

**State of exhaustion** or often called by **burnout** can be experienced by anyone at any age, not exclusive for white collar/blue collar worker. Even can be burnout if the teacher or their parents give too much pressure to study or setting unrealistic passing grade.

&mdash; Burnout is not just a feeling, it is a real physical pain and mental pain

Burnout on work is not simple matter, not only affecting productivity aspect but on some cases also cause some permanent physical and mental damage to the worker.

Workplaces burnout commonly occured because of employer pusing unrealistic performance target, whether it is sales target, completion target or as simple as arrival on time target can cause burnout.

## Effect on the Individual

Burnout on individual will directly impact on the individual performance degradation (work quality, communication quality and leadership quality) due to employee to having problem in concentrating, thinking clearly/objectively and controlling their emotion.

Chronic burnout eventually will also change the trajectory of employee work motivation, in many cases endeed up in resignation.

Most noticeable burnout symptom is the rise of absenteeism. They will now often to ask for sick leave, vacation leave or even trying to escape work(present at work but unproductive) by making any excuses.

## Effect on the Team

Nowdays, no one is working alone. Even with help of the most advanced AI, team work is still there and will never be replaced. Everyone will always work in team.

Imagine one of the core team member experiencing burnout, the overall team performance will be degraded, supervisor now starting to delegate the employee who experienced burn out to other team member, and eventually the team member who cover their co-worker task will also become burnout, it become endless vicious cycle.

&mdash; What happen if the supervisor got burnout? it will be devastating to the team member.

Most wellknown method to manage time and maintaining productivity / work pace is called as **Pomodoro technique**.

## Pomodoro Technqiue

Pomodoro time management is a governed working time to **avoid exhaustion** by ensuring 5 mins time break every 25 mins of working. Moreoever, after four iteration (100 mins of working 20 mins of breaktime), there will be long period of break. Usually arround **30~40 minutes**

Pomodoro will help to keep the sanity of the employee by ensuring their minds and physical body got relaxed every 25 minutes.

Pomodo technique rules (can be modified based on the needs, but below is the most standard one)

1. Open today task list from the work schedule
2. Choose ONE prioritized task for that specific time (do not multi tasking)
3. Start a 25 minutes timer
4. Continue to work with full focus and concentration until the 25 mins alarm time rang
5. take a break for 5 mins
6. breaktime rule : no screen! keep the mind relax. example: take. a coffeee, sightseeing, etc
7. continue the cycle
8. after 4th iteration, reward 30~40 mins long break (same rule, no screen!)
